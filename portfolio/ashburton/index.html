Ashburton

This 1920s Californian bungalow in the heart of Ashburton was completely transformed into a beautiful grand home with each area serving a purpose.

A minimalistic design ensured the Carrara marble and ravine joinery were the standout heroes while re-instating the character of the home by keeping the dental cornicing, original lead-light windows and whitewashed original hardwood flooring.

Interior design details were completed with specification of lighting, furniture, accessories and artwork throughout, allowing us to provide a full end-to-end service.
